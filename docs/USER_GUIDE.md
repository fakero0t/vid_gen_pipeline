## The 4-Step Workflow

Every video project follows the same 4-step process. You can navigate back and forth between steps using the visual step indicator at the top of the page.

### ðŸ“ Step 1: Vision & Brief (Chat)

**Goal:** Define your video concept through conversation with AI

**What happens here:**
1. Chat with the AI assistant about your video idea
2. Discuss:
   - Product/service you're promoting
   - Target audience
   - Key messages and selling points
   - Desired tone and style
   - Call-to-action

**Example conversation:**
```
You: I want to create a video for our new smartwatch.
     It's designed for fitness enthusiasts and tracks sleep, 
     heart rate, and workouts. Modern, energetic vibe.

AI: Great! Let me capture those details. What's your 
    primary call-to-action?

You: Visit our website to pre-order.

AI: Perfect! I've created your creative brief.
```

**When ready:**
- Click **"Continue to Mood Selection"** to proceed to Step 2

**Your progress saves automatically** - you can return to this step anytime.

---

### ðŸŽ¨ Step 2: Mood Selection

**Goal:** Choose the visual style for your video

**What happens here:**
1. AI generates **3 mood boards** based on your creative brief
2. Each mood board shows:
   - A sample image reflecting the style
   - Mood name (e.g., "Energetic Neon", "Minimalist Clean")
   - Style description
3. Preview each option
4. Select the mood that best matches your vision

**Tips:**
- Take your time - this choice affects all scene visuals
- Consider your brand guidelines
- Think about your target audience's preferences

**When ready:**
- Click **"Continue to Storyboard"** to proceed to Step 3

---

### ðŸŽ¬ Step 3: Scene Storyboard

**Goal:** Generate and refine your video scenes

**What happens here:**

AI creates **5-7 scenes** (~5 seconds each) following this narrative structure:
1. **Hook** (3-4s): Attention-grabbing opening
2. **Intro** (4-6s): Introduce your product/service
3. **Features** (12-16s): Highlight key benefits
4. **Call-to-Action** (4-6s): Drive viewer action

#### Progressive Scene Workflow

Each scene goes through **3 states**:

- AI generates scene description text
- **Actions you can take:**

##### 2. ðŸŸ¡ Image State (After approving text)

- Image uses your selected mood's style

  - ðŸ”„ **Regenerate Image** - Create a new image

  - ðŸ”„ **Regenerate Video** - Create a new video clip

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### "Image generation failed"
**Cause:** Content policy violation or API timeout  
**Solution:**
1. Edit the scene text to be less explicit/violent
2. Click "Regenerate Image" to try again
3. If persistent, regenerate the text first

#### "Video generation is taking forever"
**Cause:** Video generation typically takes 1-2 minutes  
**Solution:**
1. Check the scene timeline - color should be yellow (ðŸŸ¡)
2. Wait for real-time update (no refresh needed)
3. If stuck >5 minutes, regenerate the video

#### "My project disappeared"
**Cause:** Projects are stored in browser localStorage  
**Solution:**
- Check if you're in the same browser
- Check if browser data was cleared
- Projects don't sync across devices (yet)

#### "Can't continue to next step"
**Cause:** Required information missing  
**Solution:**
- **Step 1â†’2:** Need a creative brief (have conversation with AI)
- **Step 2â†’3:** Need to select a mood
- **Step 3â†’4:** All scenes need videos (ðŸŸ¢ green)

#### "SSE connection failed" message
**Cause:** Network issue with real-time updates  
**Solution:**
- App will automatically poll for updates instead
- No action needed - updates will still appear
- Refresh page if scenes stop updating

#### "Scene is stuck in error state" (ðŸ”´)
**Cause:** Generation failed  
**Solution:**
1. Click the regenerate button for that specific state
2. If in text state: Edit text and regenerate
3. If in image/video state: Go back and modify text
4. Check error message displayed in the scene card

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: How long does it take to create a video?**  
A: Typically 10-15 minutes:
- Step 1 (Brief): 2-3 minutes
- Step 2 (Mood): 1-2 minutes (AI generation)
- Step 3 (Scenes): 6-10 minutes (depends on edits/regenerations)
- Step 4 (Final): 2-5 minutes

**Q: Can I save my work and come back later?**  
A: Yes! All progress saves automatically. Close your browser and resume anytime.

**Q: How many projects can I create?**  
A: Unlimited projects (limited only by browser storage).

**Q: Can I share my project with teammates?**  
A: Not yet - projects are stored locally in your browser. Multi-user collaboration is planned for a future release.

### Video Specifications

**Q: What video format do I get?**  
A: MP4 video, 1080Ã—1920 resolution (vertical), 30 seconds duration.

**Q: What platforms can I use this for?**  
A: Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Facebook Reels, Snapchat Spotlight, Pinterest Idea Pins - any platform supporting vertical short-form video.

**Q: Can I change the video length?**  
A: Currently fixed at 30 seconds. Individual scene durations can be adjusted (1-10s each).

**Q: Does the audio have copyright?**  
A: Audio is AI-generated and royalty-free. Safe for commercial use.

### Technical

**Q: What browsers are supported?**  
A: Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari (latest versions). Chrome recommended for best performance.

**Q: Why aren't my projects syncing across devices?**  
A: Projects are currently stored in browser localStorage (per-device). Cloud sync is planned for a future update.

**Q: Can I export my creative brief or scenes?**  
A: Not yet - but you can view and copy text from each scene manually.

**Q: What happens if I refresh during generation?**  
A: Safe to refresh! The app will reconnect and resume showing progress.
